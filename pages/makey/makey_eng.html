makey makey

makey makey is a chip that you can use to make a controller for a game or other things.

dance game controller

first we had made a simple prototype that you can use with your hands. Me and a friend made a controller for a music game that you can control with your feet. Here are some videos and pictures of the controller.

shellshock controller

this is a larger project I made with a few friends. In this project we made a life-sized controller with the makey makey and aluminum foil. the controller was made for the game shell shock. you could play it with 2 people, because we had 2 controllers made on each side 1 person could play.
